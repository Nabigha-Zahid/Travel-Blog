Featured Destinations

Paris, France

Highlights: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Seine River.

Travel Tip: Visit during spring for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

Tokyo, Japan

Highlights: Shibuya Crossing, Senso-ji Temple, Mount Fuji.

Travel Tip: Try local street food at Tsukiji Market.

New York, USA

Highlights: Central Park, Times Square, Statue of Liberty.

Travel Tip: Take a ferry to Staten Island for a free view of the Statue of Liberty.

Rome, Italy

Highlights: Colosseum, Vatican City, Trevi Fountain.

Travel Tip: Wear comfortable shoes as you'll be walking a lot!

Bali, Indonesia

Highlights: Ubud Monkey Forest, Tegallalang Rice Terraces, Uluwatu Temple.

Travel Tip: Don't forget to try Balinese cuisine like Nasi Goreng.

Sydney, Australia

Highlights: Sydney Opera House, Bondi Beach, Harbour Bridge.

Travel Tip: Visit the Taronga Zoo for a great view of the city skyline.
